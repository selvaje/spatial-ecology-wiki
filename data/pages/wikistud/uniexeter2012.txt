====== Welcome to the ESI Summer school on Open Source tools for GIS analyses and data processing ======

<note important> For people unable to attend the installation meeting, a second session of software installation will be carried out **Wed. July 11th  at 14:45**  seminar room 9 in Peter Lanyon after the seminar. Before coming, install a virtualization software at your choice (  [[https://www.virtualbox.org/wiki/Downloads| Virtual Box]] or [[http://www.vmware.com/products/player/overview.html| VM Ware Player ]] ) and bring your laptop. </note>

The hand's on training will be carried out on a virtual machine Linux Ubuntu operating System with pre-installed Open source scientific software. \\
To participate at the training you'll need a laptop with 20GB of free disk space available.\\

The [[http://www.exeter.ac.uk/esi/events/title_215989_en.html| ESI ]] summer school is carried out at the Cornwall campus, during the following training sessions:\\
