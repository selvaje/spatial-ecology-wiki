=== Travel Information ===
The easiest way to reach Matera is to fly to [[ http://www.aeroportidipuglia.it/default.asp?idlingua=1&idcontenuto=25&page=Seap+Bari | Bari Airport]], around 70 km away. Bari Airport (also called Bari Palese) is served by budget Ryanair, Easyjet, Gemarwings, etc (see the [[http://www.aeroportidipuglia.it/mappavoli/bari.html |  flights map ]]). 

== Bari Airport to Matera ==
  - Taxi (80-100EURO each way, 1h15min), keep in touch with other course participants to share.
  - Bus service [[ http://pugliairbus.aeroportidipuglia.it | Pugliairbus]], from Bari Airport to Matera, running two times a day - book online in advance. 
  - City-Bus public transport [[ http://www.amtab.it/ | AMTAB BUS 16 ]], connecting the Airport with the Bari Centrale railway station. 
  - Railway line [[ http://treni.aeroportidipuglia.it/Default.aspx | Ferrotramviaria S.p.A ]] connecting Bari Aeroport to Bari Centrale Station.
  - Railway line,[[ http://ferrovieappulolucane.it/en/ | Ferrovie Appule Lucane ]] connecting Bari central station to Matera. 

Plan a 3 hours trip from Bari Aeroporto to Matera, especially if you do the bus+train option. Plane in advance your journey and consider that on **Sunday some public transportation lines do not operate**. 

== Alternative Routs to Matera == 
You can fly to Rome, Naples and connect to Matera:
  - There is a bus service [[ http://www.marozzivt.it |  Marozzi  ]] which connect Main Italian Cities to Matera. 
  - [[http://www.sitabus.it/ | SITA]] operates bus services from Matera to Taranto and Metaponto and to other local towns and villages. 
  - Use this browser engine to check for potential bus routes. 

If you want visit the surrounding of Matera and the nearby Puglia region, public transport is a feasible option. However, for exploring at your leisure, and seeing more of the remote Basilicata region, a hire car (at Bari Airport) will be extremely useful.
