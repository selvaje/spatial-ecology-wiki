====== Quantum Gis ======
Qgis is a user friendly Open Source Geographic Information System licensed under the GNU General Public License.
Main features: View and overlay vector and raster data in different formats and projections without conversion to an internal or common format. You can use it as GUI for GRASS Gis.

== Qgis as Grass GUI ==
For using Qgis as GRASS GUI, Open a Grass section and type in the Bash terminal
   qgis &
